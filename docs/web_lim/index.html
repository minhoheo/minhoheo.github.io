I am an Associate Professor in the Kim Jaechul School of Artificial Intelligence at Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (KAIST).

Previously, I was an assistant professor at the University of Southern California (USC). Before that, I completed my PhD at Massachusetts Institute of Technology under the guidance of Professor Antonio Torralba, and also had a half-year long postdoc under Professor William Freeman and a year long postdoc under Professor Fei-Fei Li at Stanford University. I received my bachelor degree at the University of California - Berkeley, where I worked in the Computer Vision lab under the guidance of Professor Jitendra Malik. I also have spent time at Microsoft Research, Adobe Creative Technologies Lab, and Google.
